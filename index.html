🌟 About Smart Study Planner

Smart Study Planner is designed to help students manage their study schedules efficiently. You can:

  • 📝 Add and organize tasks by subjects and priority.
  • 📋 View and edit tasks for each day.
  • 📅 Plan your week effectively.
  • 📄 Export your tasks to a PDF for reference.
